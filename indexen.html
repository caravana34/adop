Canines

The dog (Canis familiaris or Canis lupus Familiaris), depending on if it is considered a species by its own right or subspecies of wolf), is called a domestic dog or can. The dog is famous because is call the best friend of man, this smart and loyal animal has been associated with humanity along millenniums, in the world exits great variety of races determined by shape and hair's dog..

The dogs are characterized by having an excellent sense of smell and good sense of hearing, are animals omnivores, that is eat all classes of food, since meat until fruits and vegetables, some races been partners to the humans since remotes times, this association between humans and canines allowed the adaptation of these animals to the total domestication to human lifestyle.

At home, you should try to feed and provide balanced care for these animals, which have become essential members of many families, caring for or being in charge of a dog implies providing emotional and physical care:

- Kind treatment, provide affection and care.

-Healthy food according to the caloric needs of the pet and its lifestyle.

- Take a walk or allow free movement for their physical development.

- Clean regularly and properly.
